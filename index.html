All About Animals

Lions

Lions are known as the "Kings of the Jungle." They are big cats that live in Africa and parts of Asia. Lions are social animals and live in groups called prides.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest land animals. They have big ears, long trunks, and tusks. They live in Africa and Asia and are known for their intelligence and strong family bonds.

Tigers

Tigers are powerful predators found in Asia. They are known for their beautiful orange and black striped fur. Tigers are solitary animals and prefer to live alone.
